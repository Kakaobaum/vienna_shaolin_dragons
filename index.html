About Us

The Vienna Shaolin Dragons is a dedicated martial arts school bringing the ancient traditions of Shaolin Kung Fu to Vienna. Our mission is to teach authentic Shaolin techniques, promote physical fitness, mental discipline, and spiritual growth.

We practice traditional forms, self-defense techniques, and the philosophical principles that have been passed down through generations of Shaolin masters.

Training Programs

Beginners

Start your journey with fundamental stances, basic movements, and introduction to Shaolin philosophy.

Intermediate

Develop your skills with advanced forms, weapons training, and sparring techniques.

Advanced

Master complex forms, teach others, and deepen your understanding of Shaolin traditions.
